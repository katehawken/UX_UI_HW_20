Blue Cross Animals Redesign

Non-for-Profit Redesign

2021 UX/UI Case Study

Duration: 3 Weeks

Project Overview

Blue Cross Animals create a safe, compassionate and respected environment for all animals. We found that the current website wasn’t currently meeting the primary needs of stakeholders and users. We wanted to enable better user efficiency, usability and accessibility. Scroll to find out how we did just that!

My Role

The team and I worked through every step together.

Our User

Our Solutions

Designed A Quiz

From our user research we found that people tend to be superficial when looking for a pet and often have unrealistic expectations about their personalities and behavior when adopting.

So to flip that around we designed a quiz that learns more about the user.

This then generates a list of pets that are most suitable for their lifestyle.

Enhanced the process - Clean and Simple.

Users advised they would only consider using credible websites for adoption and donation. We took extra care to make the donation process as clean and simple as possible.

This included making:

  • Donation categories
  • Simple step by step guides
  • Clear payment buttons users are familiar with

    • Easy Access to Information

    The information articles were scattered all over the website. To make things easier for our users we added:

  • A search bar for our users to find information
  • Efficient scrolling through article tiles
  • Filters for database tags, such as dog or cat, training..

    • The Problem

    The current website wasn’t currently meeting the primary needs of stakeholders and users.

    It scored a 3.5 out of 7!

    We found this was because of the distorted images, inconsistent sizes and colours of the fonts throughout the website.

    Our users were getting lost and we knew we needed to help build their confidence back.
