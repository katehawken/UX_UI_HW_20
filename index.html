Designed A Quiz

From our user research we found that people tend to be superficial when looking for a pet and often have unrealistic expectations about their personalities and behavior when adopting.

So to flip that around we designed a quiz that learns more about the user.

This then generates a list of pets that are most suitable for their lifestyle.
